For this trial it is important to know that victims of burglary will have a dedicated investigator from the PCU. The investigator is the victims point of contact around the progress of the case and they will manage expectations.

When engaging or contacting the victim it is your role to support, and reassure them with positive messages.

This can also be an opportunity to assess the venue for risk of repeat victimisation  or key enablers that make them more attractive to crime. (i.e. protected characteristics)

At this point you may want to employ some crime prevention tactics which can be found on the APP or visit Police.uk

Don’t forget to let the victim know about the victim codes of practice which outlines our commitment to them and the communities of London.
