MENU

Cocooning or Super Cocooning is a reactive strategy to protect against the reoccurrence of residential burglary.

The hypothesis based approach started back in the mid 1990’s as part of a crime prevention study in GMP.

In this pilot the burglary project will support you with your cocooning activities by providing you with standardised material and aide memoirs to ensure we have consistent messages and engagement with your communities

Key points to remember about cocooning :

Works best between 24-48 hrs

Don’t forget to visit the victim!!!.

Aim to visit :

5 premises either side,

10 opposite

5 behind where the burglary has happened.

﻿﻿Don’t forget how you interact with the community is very effective.
